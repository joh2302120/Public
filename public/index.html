Running Out Of Everything Always

My idea for a website would be a collection of common recipe substitutes if someone runs out something.

Substitutes for One Egg

1 teaspoon of baking soda, one tablespoon of vinegar or
1/4 cup of applesauce or
1/4 cup yogurt or
Whirl 1/4 cup silken tofu in a blender, adding water until smooth or
1/4 cup mashed banana or
Stir together one tablespoon ground flaxseed in 3 tablespoons water until thick and gelatinous

I would also list some simple spice blend recipes.
Pumpkin Spice Recipe: 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg, ¼ teaspoon ground ginger,⅛ teaspoon ground cloves.
This is a pretty easy recipe to make if you have a good selection of spices at home and want to make something fall themed.
